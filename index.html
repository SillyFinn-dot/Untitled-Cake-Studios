Welcome to the Studio

Welcome! Untitled Cake Studios is a place where creativity comes first. Whether you are a kid who loves playing games or an adult who appreciates clean, thoughtful design, you belong here. We build things that are simple, fun, and built to last.

Our Goal

Our studio was founded on the idea that games should be fun above all else. We focus on smooth movement, clear goals, and making sure that every project we work on feels special. We don't just make games—we build worlds for you to explore.

Family & Safety

We take safety seriously, but we keep it simple. Our focus is on creating environments that are welcoming to all ages.

  • Safe Play: All games are designed to be friendly and secure.
  • Pure Fun: No complicated menus, no hidden tricks—just great gameplay.
  • Inclusive Design: We build for everyone, ensuring that everyone can join in the fun.
